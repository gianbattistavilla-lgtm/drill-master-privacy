Information We Collect

Trivella! does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Payment information (the app has no in-app purchases)

Internet Connection

Trivella! works entirely offline. The app does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any data over the network.

Children's Privacy

Trivella! is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from anyone, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Data Storage

Game progress (level, credits, upgrades) is saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Trivella! does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks or social media integrations.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
